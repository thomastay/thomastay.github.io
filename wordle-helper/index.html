Entering the letter followed by a period means the letter is in the right spot. Followed by a comma, means it's in the wrong spot. Else, (i.e. you just typed in the letter), it means the letter is not in the answer.

E.g. SO.AR,E means that S,A,E are not in the word, O is in the right place, and R is in the wrong spot.
